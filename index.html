Texas Choose 'Em (2021)

A new flavor of poker that's essentially Texas Holdem except players have additional agency in choosing their cards. The twist is, instead of players building their hands with 5 shared table cards, players claim areas on a 4x4 grid of table cards and build their hand with the cards in the areas they successfully claim.

My prototype has online mulitplayer. Host a private lobby and try it out with a friend! (or play against yourself in 2 incognito browser windows).

Unity, Websockets, AWS (API Gateway, Lambda, RDS), Firebase

Frictionless (2018)

Randomized physics based puzzles. Aim your shapes on a frictionless surface such that one shape touches your opponent's goal before a shape touches your goal. Get three more wins than losses on a level to unlock the next stage.

There's a leaderboard that tracks which players win (or tie) all puzzles in the least number of attempts.

Unity, AWS (API Gateway, Lambda, RDS)
