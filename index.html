Texas Choose 'Em (2021)

A new flavor of poker that's essentially Texas Holdem except players have additional agency in choosing the cards in their hand. The twist is, instead of players building their hands with 5 shared table cards, players claim areas on a NxM (i.e 4x4) grid of table cards and build their hand with the cards in the areas they successfully claim.

Online mulitplayer, host a private lobby and try it out with a friend! (or play against yourself in 2 incognito browser windows).

Unity, WebGL, Websockets, AWS (API Gateway, Lambda, RDS), Firebase
